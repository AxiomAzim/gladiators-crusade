Packing Essentials

Be prepared for your adventure in Rome! Use this checklist to make sure you have everything you need.

Travel Documents

  • Passport & travel visa (if required)
  • Flight tickets & hotel reservations
  • Travel insurance documents
  • Credit/debit cards & some Euros (â‚¬) in cash
  • Emergency contact list
  • Photocopies or digital copies of important documents (passport, ID, reservations)

Clothing & Accessories

  • Comfortable walking shoes (Rome has a lot of cobblestone streets!)
  • Lightweight, breathable clothing (pack layers for varying temperatures)
  • A light jacket or sweater for cooler evenings
  • Dressy outfit for a special dinner (collared shirt, dress, or smart casual wear)
  • Swimwear (some hotels have pools or spas)
  • Hat, sunglasses & sunscreen
  • Scarf or shawl (required for entry to churches like St. Peterâ€™s Basilica)
  • Undergarments & socks (pack enough for the entire trip)
  • Pajamas & loungewear

Tech & Gadgets

  • Smartphone & charger
  • Power adapter (Italy uses Type C, F, and L plugs)
  • Portable battery pack
  • Camera for capturing memories
  • Headphones or earbuds

Toiletries & Medications

  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, & personal hygiene items
  • Travel-sized shampoo & conditioner
  • Deodorant & perfume/cologne
  • Razor & shaving cream
  • Makeup & skincare essentials
  • Medications & prescriptions (bring enough for the whole trip)
  • First aid kit (band-aids, pain relievers, motion sickness pills, allergy meds, blister protection)
  • Hand sanitizer & wet wipes

Extras

  • Reusable water bottle (stay hydrated!)
  • Guidebook or offline maps
  • Notebook & pen for journaling your experience
  • Small backpack or crossbody bag for daily excursions
  • Snacks for the flight
  • Lightweight foldable tote bag for souvenirs

Final Reminder

Before you leave, double-check:

  • All travel documents are packed and accessible.
  • Phone, charger, and power adapter are in your carry-on.
  • You have enough cash for small purchases and transportation.
  • Medications and essentials are packed in your personal bag.
  • Your flight and hotel confirmations are saved digitally or printed.
