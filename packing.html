Packing Essentials

Be prepared for your adventure in Rome! Use this checklist to make sure you have everything you need.

Travel Documents

  • Passport & travel visa (if required)
  • Flight tickets & hotel reservations
  • Travel insurance documents
  • Credit/debit cards & some Euros (€) in cash
  • Emergency contact list

Clothing & Accessories

  • Comfortable walking shoes (Rome has a lot of cobblestone streets!)
  • Lightweight, breathable clothing
  • A light jacket or sweater for cooler evenings
  • Dressy outfit for a special dinner
  • Hat, sunglasses & sunscreen
  • Scarf or shawl (required for entry to certain churches)

Tech & Gadgets

  • Smartphone & charger
  • Power adapter (Italy uses Type C, F, and L plugs)
  • Portable battery pack
  • Camera for capturing memories

Toiletries & Medications

  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, & personal hygiene items
  • Travel-sized shampoo & conditioner
  • Medications & prescriptions (bring enough for the whole trip)
  • First aid kit (band-aids, pain relievers, motion sickness pills)
  • Hand sanitizer & wet wipes

Extras

  • Reusable water bottle (stay hydrated!)
  • Guidebook or offline maps
  • Notebook & pen for journaling your experience
  • Small backpack or crossbody bag for daily excursions

Travel Tips for Rome

  • **Wear comfortable shoes** – you'll be walking a lot!
  • **Order coffee like a local** – cappuccinos are for mornings, espresso for after meals.
  • **Be mindful of pickpockets** – keep valuables secure in crowded areas.
  • **Carry cash** – some small shops and cafes may not accept credit cards.
  • **Book tickets in advance** – for attractions like the Colosseum & Vatican Museums.
