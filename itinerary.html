Itinerary Overview

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

Arrive in Rome and check into your hotel. Evening stroll to the Piazza Navona for dinner.

Day 2: Ancient Rome

Explore the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Pantheon, followed by a traditional Roman dinner.

Day 3: Vatican City

Visit the Vatican Museums, Sistine Chapel, and St. Peter's Basilica.

Day 4: Departure

Shopping in the Via del Corso and farewell lunch before departure.
