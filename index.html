Must-See Attractions

Colosseum: The Colosseum is a massive time machine that takes you back to ancient Rome, where gladiators, wild animals, and sea battles thrilled thousands of spectators.

Sistine Chapel: The Sistine Chapel's fame is mostly based on its immense ceiling frescoes created by none other than the great Michelangelo Buonarroti.

Pantheon: The Pantheon is widely praised for its feats of architecture and concept of space.
